About

I am a PhD candidate in mathematics at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research is mainly concerned with nonlinear semidefinite programming, variational analysis, and Newton-type methods for structured optimization problems.

I am particularly interested in degeneracy and the interplay between variational structure, regularity conditions, and algorithm design in optimization.
