About

I am a PhD candidate in mathematics at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research focuses on nonlinear semidefinite programming, variational analysis, and Newton-type methods for structured optimization problems. I am particularly interested in degeneracy and algorithmic issues arising in optimization.
